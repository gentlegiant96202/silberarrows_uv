MAIN AGREEMENT TERMS
1. Consignment Period
The vehicle will be listed exclusively with SilberArrows for 40 days from the date of signing this Agreement.
Any early withdrawal of the vehicle by the Owner will result in forfeiture of the deposit.
2. Mileage Buffer
SilberArrows will advertise the vehicle with a mileage allowance of 1,500–2,000 km above its current reading (the "Mileage Buffer").
If the vehicle exceeds this buffer, SilberArrows reserves the right to reduce the return price accordingly.
3. Vehicle Inspections
A pre-listing inspection will be carried out by SilberArrows.
A final inspection will be carried out at the time of sale/handover.
Any damage, wear, or faults arising during the consignment period will be the sole responsibility of the Owner (including tyres, brakes, punctures, chips, scratches, etc.).
Any accidents, insurance claims, or major repairs must be disclosed to SilberArrows immediately.
4. Owner Responsibilities
The Owner must ensure at all times:
Vehicle is insured, registered, and compliant with RTA requirements.
All fines, Salik (toll) charges, and penalties are settled by the Owner.
Vehicle remains clean and presentable for viewings (SilberArrows will detail the car initially; service washes before viewings are chargeable upon request).
5. Holding Fee & Deductions
The Owner will pay a refundable holding fee of AED 3,000 at the start of the consignment period.
The following deductions will apply:
Missed or late appointments: AED 1,000 per occurrence
Failure to present car within 24 hours: AED 500
Poor cleanliness at viewing: AED 500
Deductions are cumulative and will be transparently accounted for.
6. Exclusivity
The Owner agrees not to list, advertise, or sell the vehicle with any other showroom or privately during the consignment period.
Any enquiries received by the Owner regarding the sale must be referred to SilberArrows.
Breach of exclusivity will result in forfeiture of the holding fee.
7. Return of Fee
If all conditions are met and no deductions apply, the AED 3,000 deposit will be returned in full and added on top of the agreed return price.
8. Viewings & Availability
SilberArrows will provide at least 24 hours' notice for viewings.
On occasion, shorter notice may be requested; the Owner may decline if unavailable.
Repeated refusal of viewings (more than 3 occasions) entitles SilberArrows to terminate this Agreement and retain the holding fee.
9. Pricing & Sale
SilberArrows reserves the right to adjust the sale price based on market conditions and vehicle condition.
The Owner will be informed of any price adjustments and must approve them before proceeding.
10. Commission Structure
SilberArrows will charge a commission of 5% of the final sale price.
This commission covers marketing, showroom display, and all administrative costs.
11. Vehicle Condition
The Owner certifies that all information provided about the vehicle is accurate.
Any undisclosed defects discovered may result in price adjustments or termination of this agreement.
